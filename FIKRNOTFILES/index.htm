With 24/7 chat/call support and 18/7 live video support we make banking as effortless as possible.

With our services, you can compare policies, insurance, interests, account schemes of different banks and choose the one that suits you best.



Our Junior E-wallet, designed for teenagers below 18, works on the credit prinicple. Each wallet has monthly limit of Rs 10,000, which is provided by us. At end of the month, the money used in the month is automatically taken from the parent's account that is linked. Parents can set the limit and other settings as they wish too. The system of credit e-wallet instead of regular topup e-wallet provides major security against thefts so that if any theft happens, not more 10K can be looted. These can also be recovered if informed in time.
